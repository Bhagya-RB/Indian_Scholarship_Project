What are the key eligibility conditions applicable to each Telangana scholarship? Which category of students are covered under these scholarships? The answers to these questions are mentioned in the table below. The key eligibility condition that applies to each scholarship is that the students must be a domicile of Telangana state only. The students residing outside the state are not eligible to apply for any of these scholarships. Besides this, the other eligibility conditions that students must fulfil for these scholarships are given in the table below.

Required Documents

  • Aadhar
  • SSC Memo
  • Pancard
  • Income Certificate
  • Caste Certificate

Eligibility

  • Class 11
  • Class 12
  • Post Graduation
  • Phd
  • Diploma
  • Vocational Course
